# Privacy Policy for FluxMarks

Last updated: 2026-02-06

FluxMarks ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or browsing history.**

FluxMarks operates entirely locally within your browser. All data processing related to your bookmarks happens on your device.

## Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

- **Bookmarks**: To display, organize, and manage your bookmarks within the side panel.
- **Storage**: To save your extension preferences (e.g., folder expansion state).
- **Side Panel**: To display the extension interface in the browser side panel.

## Visual Data

This extension does not use any third-party analytics tools or advertising networks.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
